## One-Line Description

Tawala is a simple and powerful Business Management System that helps Kenyan SMEs move from manual chaos to organized, profitable growth.

## Our Story & Promise

Running a biashara in Kenya is tough. Between managing stock, staff, sales, and expenses, many owners lose control and money every day.

Tawala was built to change that.

We help shop owners, minimart operators, pharmacists, hardware owners, and other SMEs **take full control** of their business with simple, reliable tools — without the complexity of big software.

**From hustle to structure.**

## Key Features

### Sales & POS
- Fast, reliable point of sale
- Support for cash, M-Pesa, and cards
- Quick product search and barcode support

### Invoicing & Receipts
- Professional invoices and receipts
- Custom branding (Ndovu & Enterprise)
- Digital and printable formats

### Staff Management
- Add unlimited staff (Ndovu & above)
- Secure 4-digit PIN login for daily operations
- Staff performance tracking

### Inventory Management
- Real-time stock tracking
- Low stock alerts
- Product catalog with pricing

### Multi-Business / Multi-Branch
- Manage multiple shops under one account (Ndovu & above)

### Reports & Insights
- Daily, weekly, and monthly sales reports
- Profit & Loss
- Top selling items
- Staff-wise performance (Ndovu & above)

### Customer Management
- Customer database and credit tracking
